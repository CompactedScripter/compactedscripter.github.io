No Bake Fudge Brownie Recipe!


Want to make No Bake Fudge Brownies? Let's do it!


Brownie Batter


1/2 stick of unsalted melted butter
2 tbsp & 2 tsp granulated sugar
Whisk until Fully combined !!
1/3 cup of flour in separate bowl
Heat treat flour for 30 secs
Dump flour into the brownie batter
2 tbsp & 2 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder
Pinch of salt
1 tbsp maple syrup
Chocolate chips/chunks
Mix and freeze for 10-15 mins


Chocolate Ganache


1 1/2 cup chocolate chips/chunks
2 tsp heavy whipping cream

OR

creamy oat milk
Microwave for 30 secs
Mix until it becomes ganache
Pour ganache over batter
Let it sit for 5 minutes
Cut into pieces

Enjoy!
